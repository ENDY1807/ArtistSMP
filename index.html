About Artist SMP

Artist SMP is a dedicated Minecraft Bedrock Edition server for creative expression. Whether you're painting, building, storytelling, or engineering. our community welcomes you.

With a friendly environment and robust publishing tools, it’s easy to share your creations and inspire others in the community.
